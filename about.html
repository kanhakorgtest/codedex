About

# Codédex


Codédex is the brand new way to learn to code for kids and adults alike. Journey through the fantasy land of Python, HTML/CSS, or JavaScript, earn exp. points (XP) to unlock new regions and items, and collect all the badges at your own pace.

This is currently the Beta version, so let us know what you think! (づ｡◕‿‿◕｡)づ:･ﾟ✧



# The Team


The folks behind Codédex have taught Computer Science at Columbia, NYU, CUNY, and have brought programming to over 50 million users around the world at Codecademy.

Each challenge is curated by world-class educators and engineers, and works both in classrooms and online setting.



# Pricing


The best part? The courses and projects are totally free!

If you don't want to learn by your lonesome self, we have the Codédex Club with weekly events, guest speakers, code mentors, office hours, and awesome fellow players who will help you debug whatever program you are working on, 24/7.

If you want to go fast, we also offer special Workshops with group classes and personal coaching to get you from 0 to 1, once a month. We will save you thousands of hours of being stuck in "tutorial hell".



# Report Bugs


We are trying to make this the most fun place to learn to code online.

Please report bugs and typos by tagging us on Twitter or shoot us an email. We appreciate any feedback we can get! 🙏
